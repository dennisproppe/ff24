About Miro

Miro is an online collaborative whiteboard platform designed for remote and distributed teams. It's perfect for brainstorming, planning, and visualizing your ideas.

Key Features

  • Infinite canvas for unlimited creativity
  • Real-time collaboration with team members
  • Wide variety of templates for different use cases
  • Integration with popular tools

How to Use

  1. Sign up for a free account at miro.com
  2. Create a new board or use a template
  3. Invite team members to collaborate
  4. Start brainstorming and planning!

Miro Best Practices Guide

  • •

    Start simple - Just create some sticky notes that verbally describe your idea

  • •

    Then try to verbalize the idea into one coherent prompt that could be used in an AI solution

  • •

    When the program starts to unfold, draw boxes with functions connected by arrows (with events or actions on them) to visualize the user's flow through the app

  • •

    Have a meta section where you note down the prompts you used, what worked and what didn't, and also your general progress
