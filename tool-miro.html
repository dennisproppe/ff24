About Miro

Miro is an online collaborative whiteboard platform designed for remote and distributed teams. It's perfect for brainstorming, planning, and visualizing your ideas.

Key Features

  • Infinite canvas for unlimited creativity
  • Real-time collaboration with team members
  • Wide variety of templates for different use cases
  • Integration with popular tools

How to Use

  1. Sign up for a free account at miro.com
  2. Create a new board or use a template
  3. Invite team members to collaborate
  4. Start brainstorming and planning!
