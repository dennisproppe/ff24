About Canva

Canva is a graphic design platform that makes it easy to create professional-looking designs for your startup. From presentations to prototypes, Canva has templates for everything.

Note for use

I'd advise to use canva only, if your idea needs an outstanding, individual design for the first step (most often it does not) or you want to create a purely clickable prototype without any code/software created.

How to Use

  1. Create a free account at canva.com
  2. Choose a template or start from scratch
  3. Customize with your content and branding
  4. Download or share your designs
