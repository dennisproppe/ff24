About Canva

Canva is a graphic design platform that makes it easy to create professional-looking designs for your startup. From presentations to social media posts, Canva has templates for everything.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop interface
  • Millions of stock photos and elements
  • Pre-made templates for various purposes
  • Brand kit to maintain consistency

How to Use

  1. Create a free account at canva.com
  2. Choose a template or start from scratch
  3. Customize with your content and branding
  4. Download or share your designs
